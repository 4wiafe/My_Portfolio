About Me

I started with curiosity.
I stayed for the impact.

I didn't start in tech with all the answers, just a strong curiosity for how things work and a drive to build. Over time, that curiosity evolved into a focus on development, not just as a skill, but as a tool to create solutions that genuinely help people.

Today, I specialise in front-end development with a strong emphasis on user experience, performance, and clarity. I approach every project with intention, breaking down problems, structuring scalable solutions, and building interfaces that feel intuitive and purposeful.

Beyond writing code, I think deeply about how users interact with what I build:

What drives them to take action?
Where do they drop off, and why?
How can the experience be clearer, faster, and more effective?

I'm especially interested in building real-world, results-driven products, from landing pages to full applications, where design and development directly influence engagement, behaviour, and outcomes.
